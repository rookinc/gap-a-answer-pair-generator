\section{The Gap A problem}

The current obstruction is not the reciprocal grammar itself. The grammar is exact over the realized row-pair universe.

The obstruction is the origin of that universe.

\begin{definition}[Gap A answer-pair generator problem]
The Gap A answer-pair generator problem is to construct a native candidate universe from source structure, without using realized WXYZTI pair labels as input, such that the reciprocal answer-pair grammar recovers exactly the 12 WXYZTI answer-pairs with no false positives and no misses.
\end{definition}

The target certificate profile is:
\[
\text{accepted}=12,\qquad
\text{false positives}=0,\qquad
\text{misses}=0.
\]

This is stronger than describing the realized pairs after the fact. It requires a native supply of candidates followed by an exact selector.
