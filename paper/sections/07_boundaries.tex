\section{Boundaries}

This paper begins as a generator search, not as a closure theorem.

It does not currently assert:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=2em]
\item Gap A closure.
\item A \(G_{60}\)-native generator.
\item A full G900 theorem beyond Project 18.
\item A physical interpretation.
\item Uniqueness of the reciprocal grammar.
\item Exhaustion of all native generator families.
\end{enumerate}

These boundaries should remain until the corresponding finite certificates exist.
