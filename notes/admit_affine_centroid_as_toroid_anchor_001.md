# Admit affine centroid as toroid anchor 001

## Keeper

We do not yet know the torus. We know where its chamber balances.

## Admission

The affine centroid is admitted as the only current anchor:

```text
c_X = (1/|X|) * sum_i x_i
y_i = x_i - c_X
Delta_ij = y_j - y_i = x_j - x_i
```

This supplies a translation-normalized chamber frame.

## What is admitted

- Every chamber state may be represented relative to the affine centroid.
- Transition differences are invariant under translation of the full chamber.
- Source and daughter states may be compared in the centered affine frame.
- The centroid may serve as the provisional anchor for later toroidal closure tests.
